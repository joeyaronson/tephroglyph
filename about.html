what is tephroglyph?
tephroglyph is a mixed media generative art project created by joey aronson

using p5.js, WEBGL, sketchbook, adobe illustrator, and blender, tephroglyph combines traditional hand drawn illustrations and the latest web technologies to provide a unique interactive experience

created by joey aronson 2023
